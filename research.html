Research

Currently, I live Seattle and I work as part of the R&D division in a private laboratory. The focus of my research is developing immunoassays which have application in the field of diagnotics of human diseases. These immunoassays must be efficient, reliable and cheap since they might be used in clinical settings in rural areas around the world. We strongly believe that healthcare needs to be accesible for everyone in the world.
